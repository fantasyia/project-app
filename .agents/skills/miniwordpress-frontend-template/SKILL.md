# Mini WordPress Frontend Template

## Read first

- `docs/miniwordpress/FRONTEND_TEMPLATE.md`
- `docs/miniwordpress/BRAND_ADAPTER_GUIDE.md`
- `docs/miniwordpress/TAILWIND4_SYSTEM.md`

## Core template structures

- Two-line silo menu.
- Home/Inicio button at the start.
- Silo buttons distributed across two lines.
- Sobre/Contato at the end.
- Article index/table of contents.
- Blog post layout.
- Footer structure.

## Brand-owned styling

- Colors.
- Fonts.
- Logo.
- Hover effects.
- Public imagery.
- Tone and public copy.

## Guardrails

- Do not replace the two-line silo menu with a conventional nav unless explicitly requested.
- Keep structure reusable across brands with different silo counts.
- Do not hardcode CareGlow silo list, logo or search placeholder into Core.
- Public frontend may use brand identity; admin must not.
